# Why does your business need a website, even if you have Facebook

The website is the business's own digital space. Social media can attract attention, but it cannot completely organize your offer, services, portfolio, forms and contact path. For Digital Romanian, the topic of why you need a website must be treated practically: with structure, clear examples and realistic expectations. The target audience consists of imms, local businesses, freelancers and entrepreneurs who mainly use facebook or google maps. The main intention is simple: the business owner wants to understand if he deserves a website when he already has facebook or google maps.

A good article on this topic should not promise guaranteed results. It should help a business owner understand important decisions, common risks, and steps that can be taken in a logical order.

## Facebook helps, but it's not enough

This section clarifies an essential point for why you need the website. In practice, many small businesses waste time or budget because they deal with fragmented content: a quick page, a generic form, an occasional post, or a technical decision made without a clear objective. Digital Romanian approaches these projects starting from the question: what must the client understand and what action must he take after reaching the website?

For a Romanian company, the concrete example matters more than the general formulations. If we talk about local services, the work area, the exact services and the contact method should be mentioned. If we are talking about B2B, case studies, process and credibility must be presented. If we are talking about NGOs, the projects, the impact and the way of involvement must be explained. In all cases, the information should be easy to navigate on mobile and clear enough for a non-technical person.
