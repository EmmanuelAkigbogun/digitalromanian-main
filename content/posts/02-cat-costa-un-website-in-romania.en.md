# How much does a website cost in Romania and why do the prices differ so much?

The price of a website depends on the structure, design, texts, SEO, functionality, integrations, testing and support. Two offers can have the same title but completely different deliverables. For Digital Romanian, the topic of how much a website costs must be treated practically: with structure, clear examples and realistic expectations. The target audience consists of entrepreneurs and companies who want to request an offer for the website. The main intention is simple: the business owner wants to understand what influences the price of a website.

A good article on this topic should not promise guaranteed results. It should help a business owner understand important decisions, common risks, and steps that can be taken in a logical order.
